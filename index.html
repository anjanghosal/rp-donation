Context

Background On RP Hall

During the global lockdown, RP had been a path breaker in coming with the concept of remote Soc&Cult events, which resulted in it becoming a movement across all halls and at the Institute level. It is only befitting, that we end the season on a high with a donation drive to help the RP Hall contract staff in days of Covid-19

Objective

Objective is to raise US $ 20,000 for the RP Hall mess-workers, canteen workers, cycle shed, gardener and other contract workers for the hall and send money direct to their bank accounts.

Process

1.

Creating a Team

We have created a team of volunteers in order to “nudge” you all to pledge. We appreciate that some of you may already have pledged during the Quiz. This is totally relaxed and no amount is too small. However, we think 200 pledges at around $ 100 dollars will enable us to meet the target.

2.

Circulating Pledge

We will circulate a pledge form through a link in the next WhatsApp which will have the amount you want to donate and your details. You don’t have to send any amount initially and we will run the pledge for ten days. During this time, on a daily basis we will keep you updated on the pledge and someone from the volunteer team will keep you posted on the same. We encourage you to pls pay in INR as small international currency becomes a pain to handle, though not impossible.

3.

Collecting Payments

Once the pledge time is over, we will collect the payments.

4.

Handling Donations

We have managed to close the India collection logistics. FT Cash is a well funded fintech started by IIT Kgp alumnus. They will provide us with a virtual bank account into which all the payments will be made. FTCash will email a direct link through which you can pay from your India bank acct (HDFC, ICICI, AXIS etc) or India based debit or credit cards. No charges for the same. International credit/debit cards wont work nor will paypal.

5.

Finding List of RP Staff

We will work with the present Warden and the Hall President of RP Hall to get the present list of mess workers and other staff ( we think this will be around 70 folks).

6.

Receiving Proof of Payment

Once the money hits our virtual account at FTCash , within 2 days it will disbursed directly to their accounts with clear proof to us on the same, which will posted in this WhatsApp group.
